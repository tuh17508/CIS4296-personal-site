Let's Bake and Breathe TU

I'm the Member Relations Coordinator for the baking club at Temple, Let's Bake and Breathe TU. I'm in charge of letting new members in the club. When members join through Owl Connect I add them to the Google Contact. Also I update the points sheet after each general meeting and activity we do becasue we give points to each member for attendance, helping set up, etc. And we give out a prize at the end of the semester for the member with the most points. I also attend STARS workshops to increase the number of stars the club has.
